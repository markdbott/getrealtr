Is it replacing my team or me?+

No — and this is important. Your team is still your team. GetRealtr handles the first response when nobody can pick up. Think of it this way: while you're closing a deal on one side of town, GetRealtr makes sure the next deal isn't quietly going to voicemail on the other side. Your agents still do everything that matters. GetRealtr just makes sure nothing slips through between those moments.

Can it handle buyer and seller calls?+

Yes — both, and it knows the difference. A buyer calling about a listing gets different questions than a seller requesting a valuation. GetRealtr is trained specifically for real estate conversations: listing inquiries, valuation requests, showing scheduling, investor calls, and agent-to-agent calls about access or offers.

What exactly lands in my inbox after a call?+

Within seconds: the caller's name and number, their reason for calling, key details (budget, property type, timeline), a clean summary, and the full call transcript — all by email. Unlike your iPhone's built-in voicemail transcription, which can take 20 minutes and frequently gets it wrong, GetRealtr's summaries are instant and structured so you can prioritize at a glance. If it was a showing request, the appointment will already be in your calendar.

Can I use my existing phone number?+

Yes — your number stays exactly the same. Callers dial the same number they always have. There's nothing for them to notice. We'll walk through exactly how it works on the demo call — it's simpler than it sounds and we handle the setup with you.

Does it work evenings and weekends?+

24/7 — including public holidays. After-hours is actually where most teams see the biggest lift. Evening and weekend calls are peak search time for buyers. Right now most of those calls go unanswered. GetRealtr captures them and has them ready as qualified leads by the time you start Monday morning.

How long does setup take?+

Most teams are live the same week. Setup is a 15-minute call where we walk through forwarding your number and configuring GetRealtr with your team's details — property areas, agent names, how you handle showings. No technical knowledge needed. If you can forward a call, you're set.

Is this for solo agents or larger teams?+

Both, for different reasons. Solo agents use it as their always-on receptionist — the system that ensures no call falls through when they're with a client. Teams and brokerages use it for overflow and after-hours coverage, with leads routed to the right agent automatically. The setup is slightly different, but the result is the same: no inquiry goes unanswered.
